About Us

Who We Are

Pateserie Trio's story began when Shanice, Isabel and Alejandra met through a common interest of theirs: learning to code. After multiple coding sesions and long conversations they discovered that baking was a hobby they all felt passionate about. By combining their common interests - coding and baking - they decided to create a website to share their favourite baking recipes.
